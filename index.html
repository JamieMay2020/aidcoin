Aid Coin

CAAA

Aid Coin directs development revenue to humanitarian organizations supporting communities affected by conflict.

Revenue is distributed hourly to vetted humanitarian aid organizations. All transactions are transparent and community-directed.

How It Works

Development revenue is automatically sent to selected humanitarian organizations every hour. The community votes on which organizations receive funding.

Supported initiatives include emergency food distribution, medical supplies, and refugee assistance programs.

Mission

To create sustainable funding for humanitarian aid while building a transparent, community-driven platform.
